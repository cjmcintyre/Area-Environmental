The services we provide

Our team has taken care of environmental and heritage assessment and reporting requirements for just about every kind of project, from small desktop assessments to State Significant Developments.

You can call us when you need:

  • An environmental assessment or management plan. We can assess ecology and vegetation, specific flora or fauna species, rehabilitation, cultural heritage, historic heritage and social impact.
  • High level environmental, heritage or social impact advice or management strategies for a development or conservation project.
  • On-ground assistance or people to undertake your fieldwork.
  • Peer review, auditing or adequacy advice for another consultants’ work or assistance developing a project brief / budgeting for environmental assessments for proposed work.
  • Someone to help facilitate stakeholder engagement or guide you through challenging relationships.
  • Biodiversity offsetting or biobanking / BAM (2017) assessment, calculations or advice.
